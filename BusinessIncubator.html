Our Specialities

Technology and Innovation

Specializing in supporting technology-driven startups and businesses, providing them with the resources and environment needed to develop and launch innovative products or services.

Social Entrepreneurship

Focusing on startups that have a social or environmental mission. This type of incubator may support businesses that aim to create positive social impact alongside financial sustainability

Biotechnology and Life Sciences

Concentrating on startups in the biotechnology and life sciences sectors, offering specialized resources, laboratories, and expertise to help businesses in these fields grow

Cleantech and Sustainability

Supporting startups that focus on clean and sustainable technologies, renewable energy, and environmentally friendly solutions

Fintech and Finance

Concentrating on startups in the financial technology sector, assisting with innovations in banking, payments, blockchain, and other financial services

Creative Industries

Supporting startups in creative fields such as design, media, arts, and entertainment. This type of incubator may focus on helping creative entrepreneurs turn their ideas into viable businesses
